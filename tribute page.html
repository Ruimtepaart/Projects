Johannes Vermeer

Welcome, lets see who Johannes Vermeer was and what he has created.
Digna Battens and Reijnier Janszoon were his parents. How he started his carreer is unclear, there is no definte answer on who he apprenticed as a painter.
On 29 December 1653, Vermeer became a member of the Guild of Saint Luke, a trade association for painters.
If you want to read more about this painter, you can visit the Wikipedia page dedicated to him.
